About Us


Sojar provides pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical companies with efficient, advanced end-to-end pharmacovigilance services. We act as a trusted partner and liaison with regulatory authorities, helping companies establish robust pharmacovigilance systems. With extensive experience and collaborations across domestic and international markets, Sojar is a valuable partner for all your pharmacovigilance needs.

Experience

Our great team processed more than 50,000+ cases on different Safety Databases for Top 10 Pharmaceutical companies. Exposure to multiple databases and global health authority requirements has enhanced our domain expertise.

Quick Support
