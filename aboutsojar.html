About Us


Sojar provides pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical
companies with efficient, advanced end-to-end
pharmacovigilance services. We act as a trusted
partner and liaison with regulatory authorities, helping
companies establish robust pharmacovigilance systems.
With extensive experience and collaborations across
domestic and international markets, Sojar is a valuable
partner for all your pharmacovigilance needs.

Experience

Our great team processed more than 50,000+ cases on different Safety Databases for Top 10 Pharmaceutical companies. Exposure to multiple databases and global health authority requirements has enhanced our domain expertise.

Quick Support

We provide round-the-clock services to our business partners. Our dedicated team ensures seamless support at every hour to meet urgent business needs. Whether it's a technical query or operational assistance, help is always just a call or click away.
