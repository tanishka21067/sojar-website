About Raju Bangar

Raju is responsible for delineating Sojar's strategy and realizing operational excellence. In collaboration with the leadership team, he oversees the development and implementation of organizational goals and business strategies.

  • With over 20 years of commercial and operational experience in drug development and regulatory science, Raju has held senior roles at Novartis (Switzerland), GlaxoSmithKline (UK), Syneos Health (USA), and Cognizant Technology Solutions.
  • He has deep expertise in drug safety, regulatory compliance, and global pharmacovigilance operations.
  • Under his leadership, Sojar delivers comprehensive PV and technology services such as case processing, signal detection, PSURs, risk management, QPPV services, and regulatory intelligence worldwide.
  • Raju is a strong advocate of transparency, quality, and continuous improvement in the life sciences industry.
