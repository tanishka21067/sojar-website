About Ashwini

Ashwini is experienced in Pharmacovigilance worked with across the Pharma and CRO industry, she has been responsible for leading strategic and operational level planning/optimization for Individual Case Safety Report (ICSR) management and regulatory reporting, ensuring quality and compliant safety data in accordance with global regulatory requirements working across both the clinical development and post-approval environments.

  • Prior to joining Sojar, Ashwini held positions at Qinecsa (deputed to world’s top 10 MNC Pharma company) and Atra Pharmaceuticals.
  • She is an accomplished team member who provides dynamic, strategic, and operational management and her experience in overseeing initiatives to achieve continuous improvement has proven key to meeting corporate business goals and objectives.
  • Ashwini holds a Bachelor of Pharmacy degree from Pune University, India.
