About Sojar

Sojar Research Service Pvt Ltd is a leader in pharmacovigilance, providing end-to-end solutions for pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical companies. We are dedicated to developing and implementing efficient pharmacovigilance systems, acting as a trusted partner to competent authorities and the pharmaceutical industry.
