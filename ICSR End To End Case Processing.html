ICSR End To End Case Processing

Case Receipt & Validation

Comprehensive intake and validation of ICSRs from all sources, ensuring completeness and regulatory compliance from the outset.

Data Entry & Coding

Accurate data entry and MedDRA/WHO-Drug coding for all relevant case fields, supporting high-quality case processing.

Medical Review

Expert medical review of cases by qualified healthcare professionals to assess causality, seriousness, and expectedness.

Case Narrative Writing

Preparation of clear, concise, and regulatory-compliant case narratives for submission to authorities and partners.

Quality Control

Rigorous quality control checks at each stage of case processing to ensure accuracy and audit readiness.

Regulatory Submission

Timely electronic and paper submission of ICSRs to global and local health authorities, including E2B and CIOMS formats.

Reconciliation & Follow-up

Ongoing reconciliation of cases with partners and follow-up with reporters to ensure data integrity and completeness.

Aggregate Reporting Support

Support for aggregate safety report preparation, including PSURs, DSURs, and PADERs, using processed case data.

Training & Process Optimization

Continuous training and process improvement to maximize efficiency, compliance, and quality in case processing.
