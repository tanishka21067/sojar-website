Case Management Services

Sojar Research offers end-to-end case management solutions for pharmaceutical and biopharmaceutical companies, ensuring compliance, accuracy, and efficiency in pharmacovigilance operations. Our services cover the entire case lifecycle, from intake to regulatory submission, with a focus on quality and global standards.

ICSR Collection & Processing

Comprehensive collection, triage, data entry, and processing of Individual Case Safety Reports (ICSRs) from all sources, including spontaneous, literature, clinical trials, and partner cases.

Medical Review & Evaluation

Expert medical review and assessment of cases by experienced healthcare professionals, ensuring accurate causality assessment and narrative writing.

Literature Screening

Systematic screening of global and local literature for safety information, case identification, and regulatory compliance.

Regulatory Submission

Timely and compliant electronic and manual submission of ICSRs to global health authorities (e.g., E2B, CIOMS, FDA, EMA, PMDA).

Quality & Compliance

Robust quality control, audit readiness, and compliance with global pharmacovigilance regulations and SOPs.
