ABOUT COMPANY

High quality at the right cost is a new challenge hence Sojar is one stop solution

Sojar, offers Pharmaceutical or Biopharmaceutical Companies an efficient, technically advanced and up-to-date service for carrying out end-to-end Pharmacovigilance activities. Operate as a valid conversational partner and mediator towards the competent Authorities and the pharmaceutical industry with regard to Pharmacovigilance activities. Advertise and develop the culture of pharmacovigilance in order to promote and encourage the organization of efficient and validated pharmacovigilance systems in the pharmaceutical industry. Sojar, thanks to the deep experience gained within the associative context and thanks to the collaborations with important medium and small pharmaceutical or biopharmaceutical companies, both domestic and international, proposes itself as a precious and indispensable interlocutor for the performance of the Pharmacovigilance activities of your company.
